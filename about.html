Light Cresent

We are pioneers in the fabrication of Lifting Equipment, Industrial Lifting Equipment and Material Handling Equipment, widely used in numerous industries in Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka & Andhra Pradesh. Incepted in the year 1989, “Light Cresent” is involved in the fabrication of technological advanced range of Lifting Equipment, E.O.T. Double Girder Crane, Single Grider EOT Crane, Over Head Mono Rail Travelling Trolley, Wire rope pulley, Manila Rope Pulley, Manual Crab Winches, Worm Gear Winch Machines, Pillar type JIB Crane, Manual JIB Crane, Jib Cranes, Hydraulic Jib Crane, Electrical Wire Rope Hoist, Electric Wire Rope Hoist, Vogal Wire Rope Hoist, HOT Single Girder Crane, HOT Double Girder Crane etc…
